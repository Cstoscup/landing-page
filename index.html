AKC Labrador Retriever Puppies

John Washington is from Orlando, Florida and breeds Labrador Retrievers for therapy and companionship. He adheres to the recommendations of the LRC regarding health exams and certifications. Many of his dogs are in the Show Dog Hall of Fame. John is recognized by AKC as a Breeder of Merit.

Is a Labrador Retriever right for you?

Labs love outdoor activities like hiking, camping and water sports.

Labs come in three colors: black, yellow and chocolate.

Labs are kind, outgoing, eager to please, and tend not to be aggressive.

Labs are easy-going, easy-to-train dogs.
